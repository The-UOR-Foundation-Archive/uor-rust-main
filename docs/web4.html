What is Web4?

Web4 is our forward-thinking vision to redefine the web. It combines the best of decentralization, enhanced security, and user-centric design to offer a next-generation browsing experience.

Key Concepts

  • Decentralized infrastructure
  • Empowered user control and privacy
  • Interoperability across platforms

Impact on UOR Framework

The UOR Framework integrates Web4 principles to ensure that developers can build applications that are scalable, secure, and future-proof.
